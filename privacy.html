Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Garden Vision ("we", "our", "us") is operated by TERRA EM INFO CONSULT SRL. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our services:

  • Photos you upload: Garden photos you choose to upload for AI design generation. These are sent to our AI service provider for processing and are not stored permanently.
  • Location data: City and country you select (or GPS-detected location) to provide climate-specific plant recommendations. We do not track your real-time location.
  • Account information: Email address and authentication data if you create an account.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics about app usage to improve our service.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI-powered garden designs from your uploaded photos
  • To provide climate-specific plant recommendations based on your location
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To improve our app and services

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our app's functionality:

  • Leonardo AI: For AI image generation. Your uploaded photos are sent to Leonardo's servers for processing.
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): For plant recommendations and garden chat functionality.
  • Supabase: For authentication and data storage.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription and payment management.

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your data.

Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your photos are processed for design generation and are not permanently stored on our servers. Account data is stored securely using Supabase with encryption at rest and in transit.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of analytics data collection
  • Export your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@gardenvision.app.

Children's Privacy

Garden Vision is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

⚠ Disclaimer

Garden Vision provides AI-generated garden designs and plant recommendations for inspirational and informational purposes only. This application does not replace professional landscape architecture, horticulture, or gardening advice.

Please note:

  • Generated images are artistic visualizations and may not accurately represent the actual appearance of your space after implementation.
  • Spatial proportions in generated images are approximate. The AI interprets your photo as a design reference, not as an accurate spatial measurement. The generated garden may appear larger, smaller, or differently proportioned than your actual space. Use the design as creative inspiration, not as a scaled blueprint.
  • Plant recommendations are based on general climate data for your selected location and may not account for specific soil conditions, microclimates, sun exposure, or other site-specific factors on your property.
  • Always consult with a local nursery or qualified landscape professional before purchasing plants or beginning any garden project.
  • Garden Vision and its developers are not responsible for any outcomes resulting from the use of designs, plant recommendations, or advice provided through this application.
  • Results may vary based on photo quality, actual growing conditions, and implementation methods.

By using this application, you acknowledge and accept these limitations.
